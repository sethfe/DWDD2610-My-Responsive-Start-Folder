Project Manager

Google's project manager, Abigail Davis, will speak at the web design summit. She will share her expertise in project management strategies for large-scale tech ecosystems and how web design enhances user experiences and synergy across Google's digital offerings.

CEO

Taylor, a visionary leader in the tech industry, as the founder of "Code," a company empowering children with coding skills, Taylor brings a unique perspective on technology and education.
